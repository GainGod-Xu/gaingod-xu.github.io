Dr. Hao Xu

Bio

Dr. Hao Xu is currently a Research Fellow in the Department of Medicine at Brigham and Women’s Hospital and Harvard Medical School. Dr. Xu is broadly interested in building data-efficient and interpretable AI systems to advance drug discovery and biomedical research. His research focuses on the intersection of
  • AI for Science (AI4Science),
  • AI for Healthcare (AI4Health),
  • Large Language Model (LLM) reasoning,
  • Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT) for Multimodal LLMs (MLLMs).

Education

  • 2011–2015: B.S. in Chemistry & B.Econ. in Finance, China Agricultural University
  • 2015–2017: M.S. in Computational Chemistry, Southern Methodist University
  • 2017–2022: Ph.D. in Chemistry, Boston College
  • 2020–2022: M.S. in Computer Science, Georgia Institute of Technology

Working Experience

  • 2023–2024: Postdoctoral Associate in Machine Learning, Brandeis University
  • 2024–2026: Research Fellow in AI & Medicine, Brigham and Women's Hospital & Harvard Medical School
